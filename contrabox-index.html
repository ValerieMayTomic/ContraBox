How to play

Players: 2 (blue and red)

Goal: Be the first to move your dark-colored player block to the purple exit.

Controls: On your turn, click a block (either your player block or one of your light-colored obstacle blocks) and use the arrow keys to move it. You can only move one block per turn - once you've started moving a block, you cannot select another block to move. When you are satisfied with the position of the block, hit space to finish your turn.
